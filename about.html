Our Statement Of Faith.

Belief in the Trinity

Christians believe in the Triune God—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—as one God in three persons, coexisting eternally in perfect unity.

Faith in Jesus Christ

As Christians,We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, the Savior of humanity, who was incarnated, lived a sinless life, died on the cross for the forgiveness of sins, and was resurrected on the third day, offering salvation to all who believe in Him.

Authority of the Bible

Christians believe that the Bible is the inspired and authoritative Word of God, containing truths necessary for salvation and guiding principles for living a faithful Christian life.

Salvation by Grace through Faith

Christians believe that salvation is a gift from God, received by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, and not by human effort or merit. This faith leads to a transformed life empowered by the Holy Spirit.
